# Wallets & Accounts

## Overview

Viem separates Accounts from Transports. A JSON-RPC Account asks a connected wallet to sign, while
a Local Account signs inside your application. Wallet Actions cover connection, permissions,
capability discovery, batched calls, chain management, and asset discovery.
