# Transactions

## Overview

Viem provides a complete transaction lifecycle through the
[`Actions.transaction`](/docs/actions/wallet/transaction/send) namespace. Build a request, estimate
its cost, sign locally or through a wallet, broadcast it, and wait for confirmation without losing
the Chain and Account types established by your Client.
